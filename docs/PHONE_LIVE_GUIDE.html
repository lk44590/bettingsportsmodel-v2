📱 Phone Live Access

Refresh picks anytime from your phone

Option 1: Same WiFi (Easiest - No Setup!)

  1. Double-click START_LIVE_SERVER.bat
  2. It shows an IP address like http://192.168.1.100:5000
  3. Make sure phone and PC are on same WiFi
  4. Open that IP in phone's browser
  5. Tap "Refresh Picks Now" anytime!

Note: Only works when phone & PC are on same network.

Option 2: Internet Anywhere (Best - Works Anywhere)

  1. Download ngrok from ngrok.com
  2. Extract ngrok.exe to the Betting model folder
  3. Create free account at ngrok.com, copy your authtoken
  4. Open cmd in this folder, run: ngrok authtoken YOUR_TOKEN
  5. Double-click START_WITH_NGROK.bat
  6. It gives you a HTTPS URL like https://abc123.ngrok.io
  7. Bookmark that URL on your phone!

Works from anywhere - your phone can refresh even on 4G/5G.

Option 3: Auto-Daily (No Phone Refresh Needed)

If you just want fresh picks every morning automatically:

  1. Run SETUP_AUTO_DEPLOY.bat
  2. It deploys to Netlify daily at 8 AM
  3. Bookmark the Netlify URL on your phone
  4. Phone shows new picks automatically each day

All options keep your bet tracking saved to your phone!
